The Process

How It Works

Four simple steps to a completely seamless, queue-free shopping experience at DMart Crystal Mall.

1 🛒

Grab a Smart Cart

Pick up a DMart Smart Cart at the entrance. It comes pre-equipped with a barcode scanner and digital display.

2 📷

Scan Products

Simply scan each product's barcode as you place it in the cart. Items are instantly added to your digital bill.

3 🧾

Review Digital Bill

See your running total in real-time on the cart's screen. Adjust quantities or remove items anytime.

4 ✅

Pay & Go

Tap to pay directly on the cart. Get your digital receipt and walk out — no cashier queue needed!

Capabilities

Packed with Smart Features

Every feature of the Smart Cart is designed to eliminate friction and make shopping effortlessly modern.

Barcode Scanner Integration

Industrial-grade barcode scanner embedded in the cart handles all standard product codes instantly with near-zero latency.

Real-Time Cart Display

A bright, glare-resistant LCD screen shows your live cart contents, individual prices, and running total at all times.

Auto Billing System

Bills are generated automatically with GST calculations, product breakdown, and timestamp — no manual entry required.

Queue-Free Checkout

Skip the cashier counter entirely. Pay directly at the exit kiosk or via the cart's integrated payment terminal.

Mobile Compatible

Sync your cart with the DMart app to view your bill on your phone, earn loyalty points, and enable tap-to-pay.

Digital Receipt

Receive a fully itemized digital receipt via SMS or email. No paper waste and easy access for returns or warranties.

About This Project

The DMart Crystal Mall Smart Cart is a C2P (Customer-to-Process) innovation project developed as part of a college engineering curriculum. The project aims to solve real-world retail inefficiencies by bringing smart technology to everyday shopping.

The system integrates barcode scanning hardware, embedded software, and a digital billing interface to create a seamless, queue-free checkout experience within the DMart Crystal Mall ecosystem.
